ABOUT US

Founded by a passionate physiotherapist turned hobby baker, our journey began with a love for cakes, creative decorating, and a commitment to crafting delicious treats with a healthy twist. What started as a personal passion has now blossomed into a full-fledged baking business, where we specialize in creating delectable cakes that not only tantalize your taste buds but also cater to your well-being. At Sugarplum Fairies, we believe in the power of wholesome ingredients and innovative baking techniques. Our founder's background in physiotherapy has instilled a deep understanding of the importance of a balanced lifestyle, which is why we take pride in offering a range of treats that harmoniously blend indulgence and nourishment. From intricately designed celebration cakes that become the centerpiece of your special moments to a variety of health-conscious cookies that let you enjoy sweetness guilt-free, our creations are a result of dedication, creativity, and a genuine love for baking. We meticulously source the finest ingredients, ensuring that each bite is a delightful experience for your taste buds and a mindful choice for your body. Whether you're looking for a stunning cake for a milestone celebration or simply seeking a guilt-free treat to satisfy your cravings, Sugarplum Fairies is here to exceed your expectations. Join us in celebrating the joy of baking, artistic expression, and embracing a balanced approach to indulgence.
